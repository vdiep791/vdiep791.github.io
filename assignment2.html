Assignment 2 - Getting Started with 3D printing

Assignment 2 was a 2 part assignment, Assembling the 3D Printer, and Calibrating it.

Assembling the 3D Printer

Since I had already assembled my printer before starting the Assignment, I'll talk about some intresting experiences I had with the Process.

Binding on the Z Axis

  • My 3D printer did not want to move in the Z axis
  • To troubleshoot this I had to take apart and reassemble the Z-Rod Assembly multiple times
  • I think in the end what made it work was loosening the screws by the Extruder assemlby.

Leveling the Bed

First I tightened the wheels all the way

Then I used this bedleveling test from thingiverse - Ender 3 Level Test

It took several tries to get it working, even then the axis's were still off and I am trying to calibrate it more closely still.

Calibration Prints

We then needed to print each of the following, and track the measurements and Time for each print.

  • A 2cm cube with the standard "low quality" settings and a brim
    • Time: 25m
    • Y Axis: 19.54mm
    • X Axis: 20.20mm
    • Z Axis 20.00mm
  • A 2cm cube with the standard "standard quality" settings and a brim
    • Time: 32m 11s
    • Y Axis: 19.40mm
    • X Axis: 20.20mm
    • Z Axis 19.90mm
  • A 2cm cube with the standard "high quality" settings and a brim
    • Time: 51m 52s
    • Y Axis: 19.44mm
    • X Axis: 20.0mm
    • Z Axis 19.94mm
  • A 2cm cube with a concentric top and bottom layer
    • Time: 34m 23s
    • Y Axis: 19.45mm
    • X Axis: 20.10mm
    • Z Axis 19.91mm
  • A tube 2cm in diameter and .5cm high with a single extrusion wall thickness
    • Time: 6m 13s
    • Y Axis: 19.23mm
    • Interior Y Axis: 18.21mm
    • X Axis: 20.20mm
    • Interior X Axis: 18.64mm
    • Z Axis 4.87mm
  • A tube 2cm in diameter and .5cm high with a double extrusion wall thickness and random z-seam alignment
    • Time: 7m 33s
    • Y Axis: 19.50mm
    • Interior Y Axis: 17.92mm
    • X Axis 20.32mm
    • Interior X Axis: 18.65mm
    • Z Axis 4.81mm
  • A cylinder 2cm in diameter exported with a 0.1cm tolerance, printed on its round side with supports
    • Time: 19m26s
    • Y Axis: 4.74
    • X Axis: 20.12mm
    • Z Axis 19.92mm
  • A cylinder 2cm in in diameter exported with a 0.005cm tolerance
    • Time: 21m 57s
    • Y Axis: 5.10mm
    • X Axis: 19.42mm
    • Z Axis 19.82mm

    Finally, A 20.0mm +/- 0.5mm cube with a 5.0mm +/- 0.5mm hole through it.

  • Attempt 1
    • Time 1h 2m 35s
    • Y Axis: 19.37mm
    • X Axis: 19.95mm

    • Hole Diameter 9.75mm x 9.25mm oopsies, it should be 5mm
  • Attempt 2 after recalibrating the bed
    • Time 1h 3m 38s
    • Y Axis: 19.53mm
    • X Axis: 19.92mm
    • Z Axis 19.82mm
    • Hole Diameter 9.23mm x 9.93mm oopsies, it should be 5mm
  • Attempt 3 after adjusting the stl
    • Time 36m 20s
    • Y Axis: 19.54mm
    • X Axis: 20.06mm
    • Z Axis 19.95mm
    • Hole Diameter 4.86mm x 4.56mm
    • Within Requested tolerance, but still looking wonky. Will need to do more calibrating
